## Where each course states its license

Licensing is **not** standardized across this site. Each course (and sometimes each
content type within a course) carries its own license. Find the specifics here:

- **[Data 8](../courses/data8.md#license):** the textbook and the course materials are
  licensed differently.
- **[Data 6](../courses/data6.md#license):** course notes are licensed under
  CC-BY-NC-SA 4.0.

Always confirm the license on the course page before adapting or redistributing its
materials.

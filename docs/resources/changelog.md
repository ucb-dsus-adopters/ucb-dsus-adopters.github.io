# Changelog

A record of notable changes to this adoption site.

## 2026-06

- **New site launched.** Migrated from the previous MyST site to MkDocs Material under the
  `ucb-dsus-adopters` organization.
- **Standardized adoption guide.** Setup, Canvas import, and Otter autograding instructions
  are now a single source of truth, composed into each course page.
- **Data 8 and Data 6** ported as full adoption packages.
- **El Camino College CSCI 9 (Practical Data Science) and Data 100** added as resource pages
  while full adoption materials are in progress.
- **Site reframed as curriculum adoption** - now covers courses and modules, not just courses.
- **Modules section added** with pages for Cal-ICOR and Data Science Modules.
- **Infrastructure page updated** - removed tabbed chooser; Cal-ICOR and Cloudbank now have
  their own sections with clearer descriptions.
- **Glossary updated** - expanded Cal-ICOR and Cloudbank entries.

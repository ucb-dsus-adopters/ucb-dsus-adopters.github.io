# Modules

Standalone Jupyter notebooks you can drop into an existing course - no full course adoption required.

</div>

Modules are self-contained notebooks on specific topics (ethnic studies, AI, calculus,
chemistry, and more). They are designed to fit into your existing classroom, regardless of
discipline.

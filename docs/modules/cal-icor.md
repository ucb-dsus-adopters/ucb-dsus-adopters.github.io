# Cal-ICOR Modules
</div>

[Cal-ICOR](https://www.cal-icor.org/) (California Interactive Computing Open Resource) is an effort to bring computational notebooks into courses across California
higher education.

## Materials

- **Cal-ICOR Modules Textbook:**
  [cal-icor.github.io/textbook](https://cal-icor.github.io/textbook/) - instructions for
  contributing and adopting notebooks are included in the textbook.
- **Materials share-out:**
  [cal-icor.org/materials](https://www.cal-icor.org/materials/)
- **Calculus resources:**
  [github.com/cal-icor/calculus](https://github.com/cal-icor/calculus) - Jupyter notebooks
  for instructors teaching Calculus.

## How to adopt

Instructors who use Jupyter notebooks can add their own notebooks to the Cal-ICOR
collection. The [Cal-ICOR textbook](https://cal-icor.github.io/textbook/) has instructions
on how to download and contribute modules.

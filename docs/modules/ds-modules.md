# Data Science Modules
</div>

[UC Berkeley Data Science Modules](https://ds-modules.github.io/DS-Modules/) are
multidisciplinary Jupyter notebooks that can fit into your classroom regardless of subject
area. Topics span ethnic studies, AI, calculus, chemistry, and more.

## El Camino College x UC Berkeley collaboration

The modules shared here are the result of a collaboration between El Camino College and
UC Berkeley's Division of Computing, Data Science, and Society (CDSS). Faculty at El Camino
College worked with UC Berkeley's Data Science Undergraduate Studies (DSUS) team to adapt
and develop notebooks that bring data science into courses across disciplines. The
[modules textbook](https://ds-modules.github.io/ecc-textbook/) is the primary output of
this collaboration, with notebooks covering topics like CalEnviroScreen environmental
justice data, AI, calculus, chemistry, and ethnic studies.

This partnership serves as a model for how community colleges and research universities can
co-develop open educational resources that work in both settings.

## How to adopt

The [modules textbook](https://ds-modules.github.io/ecc-textbook/) has instructions on how
to download the notebooks into your course. Modules are self-contained, so you can pick the
ones that fit your curriculum without adopting a full course.

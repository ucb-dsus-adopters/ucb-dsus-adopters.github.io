# Data Science Modules
</div>

[UC Berkeley DSUS Modules](https://cdss.berkeley.edu/dsus/modules) are multidisciplinary
Jupyter notebooks that can fit into your classroom regardless of subject area. Topics span
ethnic studies, AI, calculus, chemistry, and more.

## Materials

- **Modules textbook for adoption:**
  [ds-modules.github.io/ecc-textbook](https://ds-modules.github.io/ecc-textbook/) -
  includes instructions on how to download and use the notebooks.

## How to adopt

The [modules textbook](https://ds-modules.github.io/ecc-textbook/) has instructions on how
to download the notebooks into your course. Modules are self-contained, so you can pick the
ones that fit your curriculum without adopting a full course.

Adoptable course content is released under Creative Commons licenses, but **the exact
license differs by course and even by content type within a course** (for example, a
textbook may be licensed more restrictively than the course notebooks). Always check the
license stated on each course's page before you adapt or redistribute its materials.

When you read a Creative Commons license, the letter codes tell you what you may do:

- **BY** (Attribution): credit the original authors.
- **NC** (NonCommercial): no commercial use.
- **ND** (NoDerivatives): you may share it, but **not** modify or redistribute changes.
- **SA** (ShareAlike): if you adapt it, release your version under the same license.

A practical consequence: content marked **ND** can be used as-is but not modified, while
content without ND can be customized for your institution. Per-course license details and
links are on each course page.

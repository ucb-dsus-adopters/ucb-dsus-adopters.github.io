# About

The central home for adopting UC Berkeley Data Science curriculum.

</div>

This site brings the full set of materials used to teach courses and modules at Berkeley
and partner institutions (notebooks, solutions, slides, worksheets, Canvas templates, and
autograding) to instructors and institutions everywhere.

## Why adopt

- **Complete course packages:** notebooks, slides, and assignments ready to use.
- **Proven pedagogy:** refined over many semesters with thousands of Berkeley students.
- **Full support:** access to grading infrastructure, Canvas templates, and our team.
- **Open and adaptable:** modify the materials to fit your institution (within each
  course's license).
- **NSF-funded:** supported by the National Science Foundation and partners such as
  [Cloudbank](https://www.cloudbank.org/) and the California Learning Lab.

## Acknowledgments

This work is supported by the U.S. National Science Foundation and partners including

# About

The central home for adopting data science curriculum and computing infrastructure.

</div>

This site brings courses, modules, and infrastructure developed by UC Berkeley and partner
institutions (notebooks, solutions, slides, worksheets, Canvas templates, and autograding)
to instructors and institutions everywhere.

## Why adopt

- **Complete course and module packages:** notebooks, slides, and assignments ready to use.
- **Proven pedagogy:** refined over many semesters at UC Berkeley and partner institutions
  including El Camino College.
- **Full support:** access to grading infrastructure, Canvas templates, and our team.
- **Computing infrastructure:** hub provisioning through Cal-ICOR (California) and
  Cloudbank (nationwide), even without adopting curriculum.
- **Open and adaptable:** modify the materials to fit your institution (within each
  course's or module's license).
- **NSF-funded:** supported by the National Science Foundation and partners such as
  [Cloudbank](https://www.cloudbank.org/) and the California Learning Lab.

## Acknowledgments

This work is supported by the U.S. National Science Foundation and partners including
Cloudbank and the California Learning Lab. Many faculty, instructors, and teaching

modules across the years.
